# Book 4: The Ultimate Daily-Use Cheat Sheet

This is your master reference guide. These are the 20% of classes you will use 80% of the time.

## 📏 Layout & Spacing
*   **Containers:** `container`, `mx-auto`, `max-w-7xl`
*   **Padding (Inside):** `p-4` (all sides), `px-4` (horizontal), `py-4` (vertical), `pt-4` (top).
*   **Margin (Outside):** `m-4`, `mx-auto`, `mb-4` (bottom), `mt-8` (top).
*   **Width:** `w-full` (100%), `w-screen` (100vw), `w-1/2` (50%).
*   **Height:** `h-full`, `h-screen` (100vh), `h-64` (fixed size).

## 📐 Flexbox & Grid
*   **Flex Parent:** `flex`, `flex-col`, `flex-wrap`
*   **Flex Alignment:** `items-center` (cross-axis), `justify-between` (main-axis).
*   **Grid Parent:** `grid`, `grid-cols-2`, `grid-cols-3` (columns).
*   **Grid Children:** `col-span-2`, `col-span-full`.
*   **Spacing Between:** `gap-4`, `gap-8`.

## ✍️ Typography
*   **Size:** `text-sm`, `text-base`, `text-xl`, `text-4xl`.
*   **Weight:** `font-light`, `font-medium`, `font-bold`.
*   **Color:** `text-gray-900`, `text-slate-500`, `text-blue-600`.
*   **Alignment:** `text-center`, `text-left`, `text-right`.
*   **Leading (Line Height):** `leading-tight`, `leading-loose`.

## 🎨 Backgrounds & Borders
*   **Backgrounds:** `bg-white`, `bg-gray-50`, `bg-transparent`.
*   **Borders:** `border` (1px), `border-b` (bottom only), `border-gray-200`.
*   **Rounded Corners:** `rounded` (sm), `rounded-lg`, `rounded-full` (circles).

## ✨ Effects & Shadows
*   **Shadows:** `shadow-sm`, `shadow-md` (cards), `shadow-lg` (modals).
*   **Opacity:** `opacity-50`, `opacity-100`.
*   **Cursors:** `cursor-pointer`, `cursor-not-allowed`.

## 📱 Breakpoints
*   `sm:w-1/2` (640px)
*   `md:w-1/3` (768px - Tablet)
*   `lg:w-1/4` (1024px - Desktop)
*   `xl:w-1/5` (1280px - Large Monitor)

## 🔄 Interactivity
*   `hover:bg-blue-500`
*   `focus:ring-2`, `focus:outline-none`
*   `transition`, `duration-200`, `ease-in-out`
